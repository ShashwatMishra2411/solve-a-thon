About Us

Established in 1984, Vellore Institute of Technology stands as a prominent engineering institution in India. Its mission revolves around delivering top-notch education, fostering technical expertise, analytical prowess, and instilling profound ethical principles in its students.

The Hostel Committee of VIT Chennai is always ready to cater to the needs of the students to ease their lives far from the comfort of their own homes. At any time of the day or night, at least one or two staff members are on duty to cater to emergencies in the respective hostel. The hostel committee is sponsoring the complete event. Refreshments and meals during the event will also be served by the hostel committee.
